Creativity Journal By Luis Flores

The inner me is still kid at heart, I like to play video games, go to conferences like the one shown above. I like to have fun, meet new people, and learn from new experiences. An explorer.

This is my life. I believe in God. I read the bible as much as I can. I have learned so much because of this book. My life has changed completely. The wisdom from this book is what I try to live. From my actions to the positive relationships I have with friends, family, and people I do not know.

Books...When I was young, my parents did not read to me. This is because our family came from poor areas in Mexico. Throughout my years, I have learned that power is knowledge. And where do can you get knowledge. Books, bios, articles, and the web. Reading is amazing! So I call myself a reader.
