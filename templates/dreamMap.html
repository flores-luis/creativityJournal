What is Creativity?

Creativity is like a superpower for thinking in a special way. It's how you use your imagination and come up with new and innovated ideas. It's not just about what you make, but also how you think about things. Not an artist but a creator. You use it to solve problems or cool inventions. To be creative, you have to be curious and look at things in new and interesting ways (outside the box). Being creative is like being a detective, but instead of solving crimes, you solve puzzles with your ideas. You might make mistakes along the way. And that's okay, because those mistakes can help you learn and make your ideas even better and make positive change in the world. So, creativity is all about using your amazing brain to think differently and make the world a more exciting place with your ideas.
